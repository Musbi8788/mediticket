## How It Works

**Step 1 — Book Your Ticket**
Click the WhatsApp button. Send a message to the hospital. They confirm your ticket and queue number — no physical queue needed.

**Step 2 — Chat With the AI Assistant**
Describe your symptoms to our free AI health assistant. It asks you a few questions and builds a report for your doctor before you arrive.

**Step 3 — Walk In Ready**
Your doctor already has your pre-consultation report. No repeating yourself. Faster consultation. Better care.
